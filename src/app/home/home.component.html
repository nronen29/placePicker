About PlacePicker

PlacePicker is designed for travel enthusiasts who want to keep track of incredible places they discover. Whether you're planning your next adventure or simply dreaming about future travels, PlacePicker helps you organize and visualize your travel aspirations.

Our carefully curated collection includes diverse destinations spanning continents - from the mystical Northern Lights in Iceland to the ancient temples of Japan, from the pristine beaches of the Caribbean to the majestic peaks of the Himalayas.
